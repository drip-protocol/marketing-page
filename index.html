Drip Protocol is a mobile-first decentralized exchange aimed at solving the inherent complexity of decentralized finance while also preserving its usefulness to both newcomers and seasoned traders alike.

How it Works

Remove the barriers of DeFi and abstract its complications with a familiar user interface to traders.

Drip Protocol is fast and cheap.

Drip Protocol utilizes Solana’s high performance and Serum’s deep liquidity order books in order to give its users the best experience.
